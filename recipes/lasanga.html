Lasagna

Description

Lasagna has become one of the crowning glories of the American-Italian dinner table with many layers of pasta, sauce, meat and melted cheese that come together to give your mouth an explosion of flavors. Lasagna is comfort food and it's always good when prepared correctly.

Ingredients

  • 1lb Sweet Italian Sausage
  • 3/4 Ground Beef
  • 1/2 minced onion
  • 2 Cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 28oz can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 6oz cans tomato paste
  • 2 6.5oz cans tomato sauce
